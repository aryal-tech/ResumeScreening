About ResumeScreener

ResumeScreener ranks candidate resumes against a Job Description using TF-IDF and cosine similarity, enhanced by domain-aware preprocessing.

How scoring works

We extract text from PDFs, normalize compounds (e.g., C++, .NET), unify synonyms (e.g., developed/created → build), remove fluff, and then compute TF-IDF vectors. Cosine similarity gives a 0–1 score.

  • JD priority terms boosted ×1.5
  • Resume key section terms boosted ×2.0
  • Duplicates detected per run and excluded from scoring

Data handling

Processing happens on your machine/server. We keep only what’s required to show results and improve accuracy for your session.

  • PDF-only input
  • Session-scoped duplicate detection
  • Optional persistence of extracted text for detail view
